# Patent AI

"Patent AI" is a pay-per-use software-as-a-service (SaaS) tool created in the ethers of a galaxy called 4E9822B1-ChrisFiver. Patent AI enables subscribers to generate detailed patent applications sourced from proprietary and classified information including high-quality patent applications drafted by some of the best attorneys available. Potential subscribers of Patent AI include attorneys, paralegals, and their clients. Patent AI's model is hosted on servers configured to maintain privacy and regulatory compliance.
